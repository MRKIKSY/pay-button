TMT TRAVEL AGENCY

USE OF FUNDS

The use of funds is for the legal actions involved in the arrest and reprimand of Dr Ifeanyi Onukwubiri. This includes the drafting of petitions to the EFCC, Serious Fraud Unit, INTERPOL, contacting all embassies, postage via DHL to the said embassies, and the bringing down of the Facebook page used in carrying out fraud by petitioning Meta, the owners of Facebook, in San Francisco.
